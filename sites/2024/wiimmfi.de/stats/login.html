Login Statistics

Here you find login statistics for profiles, consoles and games. The summaries are printed by hour, day, week, month, quarter and year. The days change at 0:00 UTC and the weeks on Monday 0:00 UTC. All reference dates are printed in UTC.

A login is only counted, if a player stays online at least some minutes. Shorter periods are ignored to avoid counting of failed logins or other issues. The counting started on Monday, 2019-03-11, 0:00 UTC. Therefore the numbers of the very first month, quarter and year are too short. Special dates and week ends are highlighted, the tooltips tell details.

Some of the column headers give tooltips about the columns when touching it with the mouse or finger. The tooltip of the active »total logins« shows an estimated number of logins, and the tooltip of every »average online duration« shows some calculations.

The top values of different columns are highlighted by a green background. Some columns support orange bars to visualize the rating relative to the top value. If such a bar is red-brown then it is considered a global top value.
